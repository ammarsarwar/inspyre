Our Story

The whole world is facing a wave of disruption and the pandemic which has shifted a lot of what we used to call the norm. This has had a grave impact on our continent's economy. Should employment as we know it today disappear tomorrow, how will you respond? How will you thrive? The idea of employment needs to be reimagined; preparing for a new opportunity path should be a constant quest. Over the years, hundreds of MLM companies have entered the African continent to promise our people better opportunities and products. It is unfortunate that these “opportunities” have provided for the countries in which they originated and have not contributed to Africa's economies that have allowed these companies to thrive. As InspYre, we are passionate about redefining the African narrative! Africa as the world assumes is NOT POOR! Our continent is abundant in rich minerals and resources that are designed to enhance and advance our wellbeing. We were INSPYRED to allow you to finally believe in this new narrative and share it with the new generation of ALL people of Africa regardless of their color, race, tradition, or age, this is what we define as the InspYre Nation. Our company is founded by the visionaries and enlightened People of Africa for the African continent and beyond!
